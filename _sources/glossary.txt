Glossary
========

.. glossary::

    EEG
        The Electroencephalogram is the recording of electrical brain activity at the surface of the scalp.

    ICA
        Independent component analysis is a blind source separation procedure that attempts to maximize independence between components.

    MVAR
        Multivariate autoregressive model is an alias for vector autoregressive model (:term:`VAR`)

    MVARICA
        Procedure that jointly performs blind source decomposition (:term:`ICA`) and :term:`VAR` model fitting (:term:`MVAR`)

    PCA
        Principal component analysis

    VAR
        Vector autoregressive model (VAR) or multivariate autoregressive model (:term:`MVAR`).
