Glossary¶

EEG¶

The Electroencephalogram is the recording of electrical brain activity at the surface of the scalp.

ICA¶

Independent component analysis is a blind source separation procedure that attempts to maximize independence between components.

MVAR¶

Multivariate autoregressive model is an alias for vector autoregressive model (VAR)

MVARICA¶

Procedure that jointly performs blind source decomposition (ICA) and VAR model fitting (MVAR)

PCA¶

Principal component analysis

VAR¶

Vector autoregressive model (VAR) or multivariate autoregressive model (MVAR).
